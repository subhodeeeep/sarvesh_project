Community Service

Community service is a non-paid activity that benefits a community or its institutions. It can be done by individuals or groups of people, and can be a regular or occasional activity.

Volunteer

Volunteering can help you engage with your community and make a difference in areas that interest you. You can volunteer at a local library, clean up trash, or help community members in need.

Attend community meetings

Community meetings are a great place to learn about what's going on in your community and meet new people.

Build relationships

Building relationships with members of your community is important for creating a community of difference.

Encourage civic responsibility

Volunteering and community service can help encourage civic responsibility.

Bring people together

Community service can bring people from different backgrounds together to work toward a common goal.

Improve your mood

Working with animals can help improve your mood and reduce stress and anxiety.

Empower others

Making a difference

Community service can help people make a difference in the world and improve the community they live in.
